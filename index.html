Pangolins!

Pangolins are mammals though many believe they are reptiles. Pangolins are the only mammal wholly covered in scales!

If threatened pangolins will immediatly curl into a tight ball and will use their sharp-scaled tails to defend themselves

A pangolins diet consists of ants, termites and larvae. They are also know as "the scaly anteater".As they have no teeth pangolins pick up food with their sticky tongues which can sometimes reach lengths greater than the animal’s body. The scientific name of a pangolin is Pholidota. Pangolins are genrally shy animals and will not attack humans.

there are 8 species of pangolin including the chinese, sunda, long-tailed and indian pangolin.100,000 pangolin are hunted and sold every year

The Manis pentadactyla which is the scientific name of the chinese pangolin is an insectivore they are crittically endangered. like all pangolins they resemble an armadillo in looks and an anteater in behavior but they are more closely related to cats and bears than an anteater.Chinese pangolins are nocturnal solitary animals that spend most of their time on the ground but they are also good climbers.. Pangolins live alone except when it comes time to mate. Females typically bear a single offspring at a time Chinese pangolin males which are much bigger than the females stay with the family until the baby is weaned. A baby pangolin will ride on its mother’s back.
